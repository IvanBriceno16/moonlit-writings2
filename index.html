Some of my favorite writers

Nietzsche

My all time favorite writer. His moral views on what is right and wrong, good and evil, revolutionize the way you think about how to act in this world. He broke molds in his day by looking back at history, looking at the world around him, and being a mad man.

Emil Cioran

Darkest writer I've come across. His words hold back no punches and come with a sense of reality that you won't find in many other people. Reading him is a breath of fresh air from all the superficial positivity that we find in modern media.

John Dewey

Our education system is broken. People hate school and that leads to people hating reading and learning. Dewey writes extensively on how we can fix this problem. His writing flows with ease and is easy to understand. He's an amazing scholar that has written on so many topics that I never know which of his books to pick out next.

"The most important attitude that can be formed is that of a desire to go on learning" - John Dewey
